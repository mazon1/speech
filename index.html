What Is a Speech Purpose?

Your speech purpose answers:

  • What do I want the audience to think?
  • What do I want them to feel?
  • What do I want them to do?

The Purpose Statement Formula

At the end of my speech, my audience will ________.
Examples:
"Understand the value of public speaking"
"Feel confident about trying Table Topics"
"Commit to preparing their next speech"
